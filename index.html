What is Kali Linux!

Kali Linux 

is a Debian-based Linux distribution designed for digital forensics, penetration testing, and cybersecurity tasks.
It is widely used by ethical hackers and security professionals for tasks such as vulnerability assessment, network analysis, and system hardening.

Kali comes preloaded with hundreds of tools, including Nmap, Metasploit, Wireshark, and Aircrack-ng, which cater to various aspects of security testing. Despite its powerful features, Kali Linux is not recommended for beginners or general-purpose use due to its specialized nature.
